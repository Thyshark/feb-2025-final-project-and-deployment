Our Story

Providing quality school supplies to students and educators since 1995

Our Mission

To equip students and educators with high-quality, affordable supplies that enhance learning and creativity while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and community support.

Our Journey

What began as a small neighborhood school supply shop has grown into a trusted provider for schools and families throughout the region.

1995

Founded as a small retail store serving local schools

2005

Launched our first e-commerce website

2012

Became the official supplier for 15 school districts

2020

Expanded our product line to include eco-friendly options

2023

Opened our second retail location and warehouse

Our Values

Quality Products

We carefully select durable, reliable supplies that withstand daily classroom use.

Eco-Friendly

Community Support

Affordable Prices

Special educator discounts and bulk pricing for schools.
