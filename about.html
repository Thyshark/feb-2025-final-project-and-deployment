About Our School

Our Mission

To provide a challenging and supportive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility.

Our History

Founded in 1995, Greenwood High School has been serving the community for over 25 years. What began as a small neighborhood school has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving over 1,200 students annually.

1995

School founded with 150 students

2002

Science wing added to campus

2010

Awarded Blue Ribbon School status

2020

Completed major campus renovation
